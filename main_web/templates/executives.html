Board of Directors & Executives

Peter Sullivan

Chairman of the Board of Directors
Byrnecut Group Finance Director / Shareholder

Peter Sullivan (B.Comm) joined Byrnecut in January 2000 as Group Financial Controller and Company Secretary. Peter has overall accountability for the group's finance, payroll, accounts, supply, logistics, treasury, risk, information systems, and systems development.

Prior to Byrnecut, Peter was a Regional Manager at RSM chartered accountants in the Kalgoorlie office, with a wide range of clients including primary producers, retail, mining, mining contracting, and Native Title.

Baatar Uuganbayar

Chief Executive Director / Shareholder / Board Member

Baatar Uuganbayar brings a wealth of experience across the mining, industrial services, and steel fabrication sectors, having held a diverse range of technical, operational, and executive leadership roles in both Mongolia and abroad.

As Chief Executive Director and Shareholder of Murray Mining Services, Baatar has been instrumental in positioning the company as one of Mongolia’s leading independent steel fabrication and engineering service providers.

Baatar is deeply committed to creating long-term value in Mongolia’s heavy industry sector. He has played a pivotal role in forming strategic partnerships with global OEMs, while expanding the company’s capabilities in engineering innovation.

Jargalsaikhan Enkhbayar

Chief Operating Officer (COO) / Board Member

As Chief Operating Officer of Murray Mining Services, Jargalsaikhan Enkhbayar is responsible for driving the company’s core operations, overseeing project delivery, and ensuring the efficient execution of engineering and fabrication activities across all divisions.

With a strong foundation in mechanical engineering and over a decade of hands-on experience in the mining and heavy industrial sectors, Jargalsaikhan brings deep technical knowledge and operational expertise to the leadership team.

Jargalsaikhan is recognized for his strategic mindset, process-driven leadership, and ability to build high-performing teams. He works closely with the CEO and department leaders to align operational goals with broader business objectives.

Amarjin Nemekhbayar

Non-Executive Director / Board Member

Mr. Amarjin Nemekhbayar serves as a respected Non-Executive Director at Murray Mining Services, bringing over a decade of experience in the private sector and a distinguished reputation as a public figure.

Renowned for his credibility, strategic insight, and business acumen, Amarjin plays a key role in guiding the company’s external engagement and long-term growth strategy. His primary responsibilities include identifying and attracting investment opportunities, facilitating high-value contracts, and leveraging his extensive professional network to support the company’s expansion.

With a proven track record in fostering sustainable partnerships and promoting strong business ethics, Mr. Amarjin contributes both integrity and influence to the boardroom. His contributions are vital to ensuring Murray Mining Services remains a trusted and forward-thinking player in the Mongolian industrial sector.
