May 1, 2018

Establishment

Murray Mining Services was established by Murray Engineering Pty Ltd, one of the sister companies of the Byrnecut Group. This marked the beginning of bringing world-class Australian standards to the Mongolian sector.

2019

Major Expansion

The first investment package was approved, allowing us to expand our operation capacity. We successfully grew our Steel Fabrication capabilities to be 2x bigger to meet increasing demand.

2020

Oyu Tolgoi Contract

We secured a significant milestone by contracting over $1 Million USD with the OEM manufacturer of the Oyu Tolgoi project (Rio Tinto Group).

2021

Manpower Supply Launch

Commenced one of the main duties of contract mining operations: Manpower Supply for multinational company projects. This diversified our service portfolio significantly.
