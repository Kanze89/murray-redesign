Murray Mining Services LLC is one of the largest independent full-line steel fabricators in Mongolia based in Ulaanbaatar city, invested by Murray Engineering Pty Ltd.

We are within the Byrnecut Group in Australia.

The Company has been developing Australian welding and Steel structure standards such as AS/NZS 1554.1:2014 and AWS D14.1/D14.1M:2005, tied up with Mongolian MNS standards.

Our Dedicated Team

We proudly implement international standards for steel fabrication, welding, electrical, sandblasting, painting manufacturing, engineering and third-party QA control / non-destructive testing into the Mongolia mining and infrastructure sector.

We have professional mechanics, welders, and electricians trained to Australian standards. Our professional employees handle projects from design until the final product. We will manufacture tailor-made solutions for your specific requirements.
